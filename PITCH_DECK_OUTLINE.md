## Presentation Tips

**Deck Design**:
- Use consistent brand colors (recommend: Navy blue + teal gradient)
- Modern, clean design (Canva, Pitch, or PowerPoint)
- High-quality screenshots of product
- Data visualizations (charts, graphs)
- Minimal text per slide (use speaker notes)

**Delivery**:
- 10-15 minutes for pitch (20 slides)
- 5 minutes for Q&A
- Practice demo separately (5-7 minutes)
- Have appendix ready for deep dives

**What Investors Want to See**:
1. Problem is real and significant
2. Solution is unique and defensible
3. Market is large and growing
4. Team can execute
5. Path to profitability is clear
6. Unit economics are strong

**Follow-Up Materials**:
- Executive summary (1-pager)
- Detailed financial model (Excel)
- Product roadmap (Gantt chart)
- Customer testimonials (PDF)
- Demo video (Loom or YouTube)
